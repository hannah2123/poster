Sweets Sale

Where: Edgewood College

When: Wednesday, June 19 at 8:30am-4:00pm

Come to our bake sale! You will enjoy our wide variety of treats. We will have slices of cake, muffins, croissants, donuts, cookies, and cupcakes for $3 each. Popsicles, ice cream, smoothie, slushies, and snow cones are $2 each. Soda, cold water, juice, iced tea, and lemonade will be $1 each. You will be happy to buy one of our delicious treats when you learn that each and every product is made from scratch. The money will be going to local animal shelters. While at the sweet sale, you might meet one of the puppies from the shelter. The puppies will be at the sweet sale between the hours of 12am and 2pm. Volunteers from the shelter will be there, selling the goods. So please stop by, for the delicious food, or for the adorable pets. The shelter is greatful for your generous donations.
